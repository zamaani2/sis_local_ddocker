Help Information
Authority Signatures

Authority signatures are used on official documents such as report cards and certificates to authenticate them.

Authority Types
Headmaster/Principal The head of the school
Academic Headmaster The head of academics
Deputy Headmaster The deputy head of the school
Examination Officer The officer in charge of examinations
Other Authority Any other authority figure
Important: For each authority type, you can have multiple signatures, but only one can be active at a time.
Note: Signature images should be clear and professional. Recommended format: PNG or JPG, maximum 500KB.
