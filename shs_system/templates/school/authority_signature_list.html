About Authority Signatures

Authority signatures are used on official documents such as report cards and certificates. You should add signatures for:

  • Headmaster/Principal - The head of the school
  • Academic Headmaster - The head of academics
  • Deputy Headmaster - The deputy head of the school
  • Examination Officer - The officer in charge of examinations
  • Other Authority - Any other authority figure whose signature may be needed
For each authority type, you can have multiple signatures, but only one can be active at a time.
